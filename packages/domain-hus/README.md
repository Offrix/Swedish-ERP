# @swedish-erp/domain-hus

HUS (ROT/RUT) runtime for FAS 10.3:

- HUS case lifecycle from draft to paid, claimed, paid out and recovered
- deterministic split between labor-based reduction and customer share
- claim drafting, submission deadline control, decision history and payout tracking
- post-payout credit adjustment and recovery handling
- audit trail across classification, payments, claim status changes and recoveries
